Welcome To           PETCY

Hello to our beloved place for pets help, here you can find your pet to take care of it and share some mercy with it.

KNOW US BETTER

01

Petcy name

We wanted the name of the system to be simple yet meaningful, it is actually composed of two words pet and mercy since we believe these two words have a strong connection.



02

Community

The system is more like a community that facilitates several services for users regarding their pets, and also stray pets that need assistance it also facilitates the communication between users and with veterinarians.


03

Charity platform

Petcy is a charity platform that provides help to pets and their owners, not only that but Petcy makes sure that every pet can find a proper safe loving home and medical assistance from our volunteered veterinarians.
